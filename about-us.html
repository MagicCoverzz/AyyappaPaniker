About Us

Ayyappa Paniker Foundation is a cultural organisation based in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. The main objective of the foundation is to continue and promote the creative and cultural spirit of Ayyappa Paniker - to explore the possibilities of language, to idenitfy and nurture young poets, to promote research into Malayalam and other literary cultures, and to sensitise human minds to the suffering of others. Towards this end, the Ayyappa Paniker Foundation organises symposia and seminars, coducts workshops and edits and publishes journals and books.
