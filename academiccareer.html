Academic Career

 
Teaching and Research

 

1951-52

Lecturer in English at the CMS College, Kottayam

1952

Lecturer in English at the Mahatma Gandhi College, Trivandrum

1952-65

Lecturer in English at the University College, Thiruvananthapuram

1966

Obtains certificate from the Institute of English, Hyderabad.

1969

Fulbright Fellowship

1971

AM and PhD Degrees from Indiana University, USA. Doctoral dissertation: 'Robert Lowell: The Development of His Poetry', under the supervision of Robert E Gross

1974-80

Reader at the Institute of English

1980

Becomes Professor and Head of the Dept of English, University of Kerala.

1980-90

Dean, Faculty of Arts, University of Kerala

1981-82

Post-doctoral Research at Yale and Harvard Universities

1984-85

Selected as the National Lecturer by the UGC.

1984-85

Gives lectures at Gorakhpur, Kolhapur, Karnataka and Bangalore Universities.
Serves as Visiting Fellow at University of Kashmir and Jadavpur University.

1990

Retires as Professor and Head of the Dept of English, University of Kerala.

1990-94

Serves as Chief Editor, Medieval Indian Literature, Sahitya Akademi.

1996

President, All India English Teachers Conference

1998

Serves as Chief Editor, Encylopedia of Indian Literature, Sahitya Akademi.

2003

Awarded Honorary Membership of the Modern Language Association of America.
